## Fundamental Factors

Nasdaq 100 fundamentals in July 2026 are a blend of **corporate earnings quality** and **macro rate risk**. Mega-cap technology and communication services names dominate index weight, and their cash flows are still attractive in a world of uncertain cuts. That supports the index on dips.

The counterweight is **monetary policy repricing**. Oil above $75 on Brent and elevated Hormuz risk feed inflation expectations. Markets have assigned rising probability to a **September Fed hike**, which raises discount rates and compresses valuations — especially for long-duration growth. Fed Chair testimony and CPI will determine whether that repricing accelerates or fades.

Sector rotation is also relevant. Energy and defensives can outperform during inflation scares while tech holds up if investors treat megacaps as quality. NDX has been winning that trade recently, but it is vulnerable to a simultaneous **rates up / growth scare** scenario.
