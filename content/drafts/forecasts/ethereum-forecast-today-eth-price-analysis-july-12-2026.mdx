## Fundamental Factors

Ethereum's fundamental layer in mid-2026 combines **network economics** with **macro beta**. On-chain activity and fee trends remain a medium-term support narrative, while ETF and institutional flow data (where available) inform positioning. None of that eliminates macro sensitivity: crypto remains correlated with US equities and real yields over multi-week horizons.

This week's dominant macro catalyst is **US CPI and Fed communication**. A hot inflation print that boosts September hike odds would likely hit ETH via higher discount rates and weaker risk appetite. A soft print could unlock a relief rally across Nasdaq and crypto, with ETH potentially outperforming if altcoin beta is positive.

ETH/BTC relative performance is the tactical tell. Today's outperformance suggests traders are willing to take **incremental altcoin risk** despite macro uncertainty — but that can reverse quickly if BTC loses key support or equities fade into CPI.
