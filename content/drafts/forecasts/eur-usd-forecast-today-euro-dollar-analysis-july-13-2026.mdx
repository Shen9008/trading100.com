Euro fundamentals this week are **USD-driven more than ECB-driven**. Oil's surge on Middle East supply anxiety (see cross-market coverage) feeds **inflation pass-through fears**, which is why EUR/USD can fall even without weak eurozone data. Markets are effectively trading EUR as the inverse of a **rates + geopolitical USD** bundle this session.
